#### 2. Multi-Camera Support
- **Enhanced:** `web/app.py` - Support for multiple simultaneous cameras
- **Features:**
  - Connect multiple cameras at once
  - Switch between active cameras
  - Individual video feeds per camera (`/video_feed/<camera_id>`)
  - Disconnect individual cameras
- **API Endpoints:**
  - `POST /api/cameras/<id>/disconnect`
  - `POST /api/cameras/<id>/switch`
  - `GET /api/cameras/active`

  - Reduced CPU usage while maintaining video quality
